Introduction

This week focused on ideating and creating 2D vector graphics for digital fabrication. The main objectives were to learn how to brainstorm design ideas, use vector graphic software, and prepare files suitable for fabrication processes like laser cutting or CNC machining. Vector graphics are essential in digital fabrication as they provide precise paths for cutting or engraving materials.

Reflection

Reflecting on this week's activities, I found the process of ideating and creating vector graphics both challenging and rewarding. Translating my initial sketches into precise digital formats required patience and attention to detail. Learning to use vector graphic software was a steep learning curve, but it has significantly enhanced my design capabilities. I now understand the importance of creating accurate vector paths to ensure successful fabrication outcomes. This experience has laid a solid foundation for future projects involving digital fabrication techniques.
